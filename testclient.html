Version 5 - Rising Stars


Wow wow, we're already onto our next update after the Major Minor Update! This one is all about the Cosmic-type! Even from the beginning, Cosmic-type was lacking in options, so hopefully now with this update the amount of Pokemon, moves, and abilties available to the type makes Cosmic a worthy addition to the client!

New Moves!

  • Abduct (New name) - Cosmic Physical - 60 BP/100%/16 PP - User and foe fly up turn 1. Damages on turn 2.
  • Accretion Disk - Dark Physical - 90 BP/100%/32 PP - Light takes super-effective damage instead of nullifying
  • Asteroid Belt - Cosmic Physical - 85 BP/100%/24 PP - No additional effect
  • Asteroid Slam (New effect) - Cosmic Physical - 70 BP/100%/8 PP - Usually goes first. Fails if target is attacking.
  • Butterfly Dust - Bug Special - 25 BP/95%/32 PP - Hits 2-5 times in one turn.
  • Come in Peace - Cosmic Special - 120 BP/100%/8 PP - Lowers the user's Sp. Atk by 1. Hits foe(s).
  • Cosmic Web - Cosmic Special - 55 BP/95%/24 PP - 100% chance to lower the foe(s) Speed by 1.
  • Falling Star - Cosmic Special - 150 BP/100%/8 PP - Less power as user's HP decreases. Hits foe(s).
  • Meteor Shower - Cosmic Special - 85 BP/100%/16 PP - Power doubles if user moves before the target.
  • Shuttle Loop - Flying Physical - 70 BP/100%/32 PP - User switches out after damaging the target.
  • Sleet Drop - Ice Physical - 80 BP/95%/24 PP - First Turn: Deal Damage and fly up. Second Turn: Come back down and deal damage.
  • Supernova - Cosmic Special - 250 BP/100%/8 PP - Hits adjacent Pokemon. The user faints.

New Abilities!

  • Anti-Grav - If Gravity is active, this Pokemon's Speed is doubled.
  • Chaotic Void -  This Pokemon is immune to Cosmic-type moves. The first time it is hit by a Cosmic-type move, whenever it uses a Cosmic-type move it uses another random move.
  • Escape Velocity - 20% of this Pokemon's Speed is added to its Attack.
  • Fusion - This Pokemon's Cosmic-type moves are stronger in a pinch.
  • Gravity Well - On switch-in, this Pokemon summons Gravity
  • Hamster Ball - This Pokemon's Bullet-like move are always Physical and make contact.
  • Silver Lining - This Pokemon cannot take super-effective damage or indirect damage.
  • Stardom - On switch-in, this Pokemon makes opponent's do an encore.
  • Tidal Force - Water-types cannot switch out.
