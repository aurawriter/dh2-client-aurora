A Major Minor Update Patch Notes

Welcome to the Major Minor Update! This update is smaller than some of our larger themed updates, like New Horizons, New Start and Light the Way, and is more similar in scope to the minor updates, like our most recent one where we added Squittack and Floraune. But there's certainly alot here that make it quite substantial thus making this...A Major Minor Update!

  • If you're here, you've likely already noticed one of the changes: The client is now hosted through a VPS! This means there's no more big downloads required, you just have to go to this website on any browser! At least I hope it's any browser.
  • The changes from Pokemon S/V: The Teal Mask are here. That includes the new pokemon, new abilities, new items, and updated Pokemon.
    • Besides Gliscor learning Spikes. Gliscor should not have Spikes
    • Illuminate has not been changed to reflect their version of it, as we preferred our Light-type Pixilate version of it!
  • Three new items have been added:
    • Choice Edge: The Pokemon's Crit ratio is raised 2 stages, but is locked into the first move they use.
    • Chrome Armor: The Pokemon's Defense is raised 50%, but it can only use attacking moves.
    • Safari Helmet: The Pokemon is unaffected by terrains.
  • Two New Pokemon have been added:
    • Kitsolune - Fire/Cosmic - This fiery fox Pokemon is a good Pokemon for stopping Weather teams in their tracks, and unleashing powerful Special moves!
    • Masqutirade - Dark - A speedy mixed attacker with access to a large amount of sound based moves. Unleash the power of the opera upon your foes!
  • Finally, Light-type moves have been added to more Pokemon than just Kanto and Aurora-client exclusive mons Read the Link below to see the new moves that Pokemon learn:
